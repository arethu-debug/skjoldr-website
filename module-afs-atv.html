Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The AFS ATV Fire System is a compact suppression skid designed to be mounted on an all-terrain vehicle (ATV) or quad-bike for off-road wildland fire response. It carries a 200-litre water tank, a self-priming pump, and a rear-mounted spray bar for track-side application during mobile operations. The system is designed for use in forested, rugged, or flood-damaged terrain where the full-size rig cannot be deployed. It can also serve as a forward scouting and suppression platform working ahead of the main rig. The ATV skid uses the same hydraulic connector standard as the main rig for power supply from the ATV's engine. Currently in early design phase.

AFS ATV-brannsystemet er et kompakt slokningsskid designet for montering på en terrengkjøring (ATV) eller firehjuling for skogbranninnsats utenfor veien. Det bærer en 200-liters vanntank og en selvprimende pumpe.

AFS ATV Fire System — це компактна установка для гасіння, призначена для монтажу на всюдиході (ATV) або квадроциклі для реагування на лісові пожежі поза дорогами. Вона несе 200-літровий бак для води та насос.
