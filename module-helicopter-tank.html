Helicopter Water Tank

Helikopter-vanntank

Вертолітна водяна цистерна

The Helicopter Water Tank is a 2 000-litre collapsible bladder tank designed for helicopter fill operations at remote locations without road access.

Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The Helicopter Water Tank is a 2 000-litre collapsible bladder tank designed for helicopter fill operations. When the rig is deployed to remote locations without road access, a helicopter can fill the tank via aerial bucket drops or direct pumping from the air. The tank is made from UV-resistant, chemical-tolerant PVC-coated nylon and deploys flat from a carry bag in under five minutes by a single operator. An integrated float valve automatically stops filling when the tank reaches capacity. The tank connects to the rig's external water intake module via a standard Storz coupling.

Helikopter-vanntanken er en 2 000-liters sammenleggbar blæretank designet for helikopterfylloperasjoner. Den settes opp av én operatør på under fem minutter og kobles til riggplatformens externe vanninntak via Storz-kobling.

Вертолітна водяна цистерна — це складана ємність об'ємом 2 000 літрів, розроблена для заповнення з гелікоптера. Вона розгортається одним оператором менш ніж за п'ять хвилин.
