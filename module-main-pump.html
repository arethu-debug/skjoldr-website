Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The Main Pump is a high-pressure centrifugal fire pump driven by the carrier vehicle's hydraulic system. It simultaneously supplies the water cannon and the hose reel, with independent flow control for each outlet. The pump is rated for continuous duty and features an automatic priming system that draws water from the IBC tanks or external intake without manual intervention. It includes a foam proportioner for mixing foam concentrate at ratios of 1%, 3%, or 6%. The pump body is made from bronze-impeller stainless steel for corrosion resistance in saltwater and foam concentrate environments.

Hovedpumpen er en høytrykks sentrifugalpumpe drevet av kjøretøyets hydraulikksystem. Den forsyner samtidig vannkanonen og slangekassen med uavhengig strømningskontroll for hvert uttak.

Головний насос — це відцентровий пожежний насос високого тиску, що приводиться в дію гідравлічною системою транспортного засобу. Він одночасно постачає водяну гармату та котушку шланга.
