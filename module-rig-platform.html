Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The Rig Platform is the structural backbone of the Skjoldr Modular Rig. Built on a heavy-duty steel flatbed, it is designed for rapid deployment across all transport modes: truck, helicopter sling, ship deck, or ATV tow. The platform features standardised mounting points for all 18 additional modules, ensuring fast and tool-free assembly in the field. The platform is designed to operate in extreme temperatures from -40°C to +60°C and resists corrosion in saltwater and chemical environments. Weight and dimensions are optimised for helicopter underslung load operations, meeting standard sling-load certification requirements.

Riggplattformen er det strukturelle ryggraden til Skjoldr Modulær Rigg. Bygget på et tungt stålplan, er det designet for rask utplassering på tvers av alle transportformer: lastebil, helikopter, skip eller ATV. Plattformen har standardiserte festepunkter for alle 18 tilleggsmoduler.

Платформа установки є структурною основою Skjoldr Модульної Установки. Побудована на потужному сталевому піддоні, вона розроблена для швидкого розгортання за допомогою будь-якого виду транспорту.
