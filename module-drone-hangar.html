Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The Drone Hangar module will integrate unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) capability directly into the Skjoldr Modular Rig. It provides weatherproof storage, a rapid-charge power dock, and a launch cradle for a reconnaissance drone. The drone can be deployed within 90 seconds of rig arrival to provide aerial situational awareness: fire perimeter mapping, survivor location, structural assessment, and approach route evaluation. The hangar is electrically powered by the rig's generator module and designed for DJI Matrice series or equivalent commercial platforms. A dedicated tablet-based ground control station is included. Currently in concept finalisation and supplier selection.

Dronehangarmodulen vil integrere UAV-kapasitet direkte i Skjoldr Modulær Rigg. Den gir værsikkert lager, hurtigladingsdokk og startramme for en rekognoseringsdroner. Dronen kan settes inn innen 90 sekunder etter riggankopst.

Модуль ангару для дрона інтегрує можливості БПЛА безпосередньо до Skjoldr Модульної Установки. Він забезпечує захищене від погоди сховище, швидку зарядку та пусковий пристрій для розвідувального дрона.
