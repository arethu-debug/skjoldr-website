Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The Hose Reel module provides 60 metres of 25 mm high-pressure hose for manual suppression in areas unreachable by the water cannon. The reel is spring-assisted for fast deployment and automatic retraction. The hose is rated to 20 bar and terminates in a pistol-grip nozzle with adjustable pattern. A swivel joint at the reel base allows free rotation during deployment. The hose reel is fed from the main pump in parallel with the water cannon, allowing simultaneous operation of both outlets. A manual shutoff valve at the reel controls flow independently.
