Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The Smoke Fan module will provide positive pressure ventilation (PPV) capability to the Skjoldr Modular Rig, allowing operators to clear smoke and toxic gases from enclosed structures including buildings, tunnels, and vehicle holds. The unit is hydraulically driven from the carrier vehicle, eliminating the need for a separate power source. The variable-speed impeller delivers up to 35 000 m³/h of airflow, sufficient to clear a standard dwelling in under three minutes. The design is currently in prototype testing and is scheduled for production release in late 2025. Pilot programme participants can request early evaluation units.

Røykviftemodules vil gi positiv trykkventilasjonskapasitet til Skjoldr Modulær Rigg, slik at operatører kan fjerne røyk og giftige gasser fra lukkede strukturer som bygninger og tunneler. Enheten er hydraulisk drevet og er for øyeblikket i prototypetesting.

Модуль димового вентилятора забезпечить можливість вентиляції з позитивним тиском (PPV) для Skjoldr Модульної Установки, що дозволить операторам видаляти дим і токсичні гази із закритих споруд.
