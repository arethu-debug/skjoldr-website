Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The External Pump module is a portable, high-capacity water pump designed for drawing water from open sources up to 400 metres from the rig. It operates independently of the main pump and carrier vehicle, powered by its own petrol engine. The pump connects to the rig's external intake via a standard hose coupling. It is ideal for scenarios where the rig cannot be positioned close to a water source — riverbanks, ponds, harbours, or flood-damaged infrastructure. The unit is carried in a purpose-built frame that mounts to the rig rail or can be set down independently for remote deployment.

Ekstern Pumpe er en bærbar høykapasitetspumpe designet for å ta vann fra åpne kilder opptil 400 meter fra riggen. Den drives av sin egen bensinmotor og er ideell for scenarier der riggen ikke kan plasseres nær en vannkilde.

Зовнішній насос — це портативний насос великої потужності, призначений для забору води з відкритих джерел на відстані до 400 метрів від установки. Він працює незалежно від основного насоса.
