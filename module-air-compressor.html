Overview

Oversikt

Огляд

The Air Compressor module will allow field refilling of Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) cylinders directly from the Skjoldr Modular Rig. This eliminates the need to rotate SCBA equipment back to base during prolonged firefighting operations, significantly extending operational endurance for personnel in IDLH (Immediately Dangerous to Life or Health) environments. The compressor is hydraulically powered and will fill a standard 6.8-litre, 300-bar SCBA cylinder in approximately 12 minutes. The design incorporates a multi-stage filtration system to meet EN 12021 breathing air quality standards. Currently in advanced engineering design phase.

Luftkompressormodulen vil muliggjøre feltpåfylling av SCBA-flasker direkte fra Skjoldr Modulær Rigg. Dette eliminerer behovet for å rotere SCBA-utstyr tilbake til basen under langvarige slokkningsoperasjoner.

Модуль повітряного компресора дозволить поповнювати балони SCBA безпосередньо на місці від Skjoldr Модульної Установки, усуваючи необхідність транспортування обладнання назад на базу.
